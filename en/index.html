Dragon Riders

Chronicles of Edalom

An Long is an outcast boy from Ha Gian, a small village at the gates of the Khaz'Tiber. Thanks to his perseverance, he manages to make a place for himself on the Sky Combs. But riding a dragon is no easy task, and the training has taken many apprentices by surprise. Will the young man be able to fulfill his dream?

The Dragonfly

Chronicles of Edalom

The fur tournament is approaching and the small kings are mobilizing to get a good piece to present. Derren, hunter of the Thousand Kingdoms, accepts a job for three thousand silver shields. With his saber on his back, he sets out with other hunters in search of the dragonfly, a monster described only by rumors.

The Tournament

Chronicles of Edalom

Persecuted by her own people after having murdered the clan chief, Fury is forced to leave the Plains, her homeland. Not knowing where to go or where to start, she sets sail for the Sun Peaks peninsula, along with two other foragers from different tribes. What adventures will await these three plainsmen in the lands of the Suna Empire?
